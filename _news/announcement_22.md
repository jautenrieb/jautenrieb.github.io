I gave a talk at the [Bonn section of the German Association for Security Policy (GSP)](https://www.gsp-sipo.de/organisation/bonn) on *“Hyperschall – Game Changer oder überschätzte Technologie?”*. The event was jointly organized with the [Konrad-Adenauer-Stiftung](https://www.kas.de/de/) and the [Center for Advanced Security, Strategic and Integration Studies (CASSIS)](https://www.cassis.uni-bonn.de/de) at the University of Bonn. Many thanks for the invitation and the engaging discussion.
